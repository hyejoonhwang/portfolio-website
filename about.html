About

Hi, I'm Summer — a product designer currently pursuing graduate studies where I'm exploring how emerging technologies, particularly AI, can reshape the way we design and interact with digital experiences.

I believe the best interfaces feel invisible — they anticipate what you need before you know you need it. I'm drawn to the messy, ambiguous space where human intuition meets machine intelligence, and I love turning that tension into something that feels effortless.

My work sits at the intersection of interaction design, prototyping, and systems thinking. I care deeply about craft, about the details that make an experience feel considered rather than assembled. Whether it's a generative UI pattern or a new way to navigate information, I'm always asking: how should this feel?
