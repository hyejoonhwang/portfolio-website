Overview

What if you could text someone a song instead of words?

Sometimes a song says it better than you ever could. Music Chat App replaces traditional text messaging with song snippets — you type what you want to say, hit send, and the app finds the song whose lyrics most closely match your message. Instead of your words appearing in the chat, a playable song bubble shows up with the matched lyric, album art, and a link to listen.

The idea came from how people already share songs in conversations to express what they're feeling. This app just removes the manual step — you don't have to search for the right song yourself. Say what's on your mind, and let the music do the talking. The interface is built around a familiar chat layout so the interaction feels natural, but every message is a song.

How It Works

Type a message, send a song — here's what happens behind the scenes

The flow is designed to feel like normal texting — you type, you hit send. But instead of your words landing in the chat, the app intercepts your message, finds the song that best matches what you were trying to say, and delivers it as a rich song bubble. The whole process happens in the background so the experience stays conversational.
