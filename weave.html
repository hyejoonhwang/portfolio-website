Overview

Why is overseas expansion — something most growing companies face — still run through scattered tools and disconnected vendors?

I proposed this project concept and led research strategy, problem definition, and solution ideation from initial desk research through feature architecture. Over one semester, we took this from a question to a fully designed product.

The Problem

Companies lack a single system of record for global expansion — leading to slow execution, limited visibility, and avoidable risk.

Teams over-rely on a small set of local partners, government resources are too broad for real decisions, and critical support is scattered across dozens of institutions.

Research

What we found

The regulatory landscape is fragmented

Korean companies use basic compliance tools, while global legaltech offers advanced solutions — but none are adapted for SMEs entering new markets.

Government support exists, but it's scattered

Programs from KOTRA and GoBiz Korea offer real value — but companies either don't know they exist or can't navigate across fragmented institutions.

Companies fall into a "partner dependency trap"

Without structured evaluation, companies over-rely on a single local partner — increasing operational and brand risk.

AI can solve real compliance problems

NLP for regulatory analysis, ML for compliance prediction — proven technologies, but no platform combines them for global expansion.

The Solution

Weave brings partner discovery, legal workflows, and compliance tracking into one system.

01

Partner Marketplace

Discover, compare, and connect with vetted local partners across legal, logistics, marketing, and operations.

02

Smart Contracting

Generate proposals, negotiate terms, and finalize contracts within one seamless workflow.

03

Collaboration Workspace

Manage operations with built-in regulatory monitoring, compliance alerts, and AI-assisted localization.

Core Flows

01 Roadmap

A structured starting point for global expansion

Companies generate a tailored expansion roadmap based on their target market, industry, and readiness — turning an uncertain process into an actionable plan.

02 Marketplace

Discover and compare trusted local partners

A unified marketplace surfaces vetted service providers. Teams can evaluate partners side by side, review case studies, and shortlist candidates.

03 Proposals & Contracting

Move from selection to engagement seamlessly

Teams can generate proposals, request quotes, and draft contracts within the same workflow — reducing handoffs and accelerating deal closure.

04 Workspace

Operate, collaborate, and stay compliant

Shared workspaces support ongoing collaboration between HQ and local partners, with built-in regulatory monitoring and AI-assisted tools.
